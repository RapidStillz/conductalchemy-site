Industry & Licensing

Conduct Alchemy works with producers, directors, agencies, and creative teams seeking distinctive music for screen, brand, and narrative-driven projects.

Music for Screen & Media

Selected works from the Conduct Alchemy catalogue are available for licensing across film, television, advertising, digital campaigns, and emerging media formats.

Where appropriate, tracks can be adapted or reworked to suit timing, tone, and narrative requirements.

Available Formats

  • Full mixes
  • Instrumental versions
  • Alternate edits
  • Stems (on request)
  • Custom adaptations

Commissioned Work

Conduct Alchemy also undertakes commissioned music projects in response to creative briefs for film, brand identity, campaigns, and storytelling experiences.

Each commission is developed through a guided creative process combining human direction with modern generative tools.
