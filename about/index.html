About

Conduct Alchemy is a music imprint exploring the evolving relationship between human intention, culture, and artificial intelligence.

Philosophy

Conduct Alchemy was formed from the belief that emerging creative tools do not replace artistry — they reshape it.

Music released under Conduct Alchemy is developed through a guided process where narrative, emotional intent, and cultural context are shaped alongside AI-assisted composition.

Scope

The catalogue spans contemporary Western songwriting, modern Bollywood-influenced compositions, and cinematic material developed for story worlds, screen, and long-form narrative projects.

RapidStillz

Conduct Alchemy operates as part of the wider RapidStillz creative family, which focuses on visual storytelling, concept development, and publishing across music, film, and digital media.

Transparency

Some works published by Conduct Alchemy are created with the assistance of artificial intelligence tools as part of a curated creative workflow. All releases are selected, refined, and presented with clear artistic intent.
